About the Founder

Dharam Tiwari

Technologist. Entrepreneur. Anthropic Advocate.

Dharam Tiwari is the founder of Kartavya Technology™, bringing together deep enterprise technology experience and a conviction that responsible AI is the defining opportunity of our era. With a career spanning enterprise software, cloud architecture, and digital transformation, Dharam has helped organisations of all sizes modernise their technology foundations.

His passion for AI goes beyond curiosity — it stems from a genuine belief that the right models, deployed responsibly, can fundamentally shift how businesses create value. When he discovered Anthropic's work on Constitutional AI and the Claude model family, he saw not just superior capability, but a philosophy that aligned with how he believed enterprise AI should be built: safe, interpretable, and genuinely useful.

Dharam founded Kartavya Technology with a clear mission: to be the trusted bridge between Anthropic's world-class AI and the enterprises that need it most. By earning official partner status, he ensures clients get not just a reseller, but a strategic advisor who understands the technology at its core.
